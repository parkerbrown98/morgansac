Leonard Morgan

Founder / Owner

Leonard Morgan is the owner of Morgan's Air Conditioning. He started with a passion to help others, which grew into a blooming business over the years. Now he has taught all of his children and grandchildren this trade of life. This all started as an after-work side job, and has now grown to bigger lengths than ever imaginable for the Morgan family. Leonard is a certified and registered HVAC contractor through TDLR. Graduated from Oklahoma State University.

Myles Morgan

Lead Technician

Leonard's grandson, Myles, is a very hard-working technician for our company. He has had a real passion for the air conditioning business ever since he was young. He graduated from Tyler Junior College in 2018 with an Associate of Applied Science in HVAC. Learning from his grandfather, Myles has garnered over ten years of experience in the air conditioning business. Now our lead technician for Morgan’s A/C, Myles is registered through TDLR and has OSHA & EPA certifications and is an A+ technician!

History

Morgan’s Air Conditioning began in 1975 as a part time after-hour repair service for Tyler, Texas. We have been operating as this business for over 46 years. We believe in excellence and integrity. Over the years we have expanded our business to our sons and grandsons. We are family owned and operated!


Received state license for Texas in 1983.
